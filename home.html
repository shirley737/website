Easy Stroll

2019 ECE Design Competition

Overview

We Want You

Walk Freely !

Many Parkinson's disease patient struggle with multi-tasking, which means they cannot walk and see front at same time. As your family, Easy Stroll wants to help you.
Who Will Help You When You Are In Danger?
We will help you! Our product can send a SOS message with your location to your family when the product detects your body collision.
Have Trouble With Walking?
Our product detects all obstacles in front you. The only thing you need to do is follow the laser line on the ground. Our product provides the safest path for you. Your safety is our only concern.
Forget To Report Your Symptoms?
Our product monitors Parkinsin's patient activity overtime. You can easily find your data on our mobile app.

Technology

Laser Cue
Using 6 line lasers, "Easy Stroll" detects all approaching obstacles and navigates the safest routes for you.
Realtime Location
With our GPS and GPRS module, your family and caregiver can track your location through our mobile app, not only your current location, but also your historical location.
Caregiver Notification
"Easy Stroll" will automatically send a low latency notification to your family when it detects your walker's location freezes.
